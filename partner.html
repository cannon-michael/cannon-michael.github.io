Field Collaboration

Interested in partnering on organizational surveys or field research?

I welcome opportunities to collaborate with organizations on field studies related to employee experience, ethics, performance, communication, and work outside of work.

What collaboration can look like

Practical, professional, and respectful of organizational constraints

  • Employee surveys conducted with clear communication and minimal disruption
  • Projects tailored to relevant workplace questions when appropriate
  • Confidential handling of organizational and participant information
  • Thoughtful coordination around timing, access, and feasibility

Why organizations participate

Research partnerships can support both scholarship and practice

Organizations sometimes participate because they value evidence-based insight, want to contribute to research, or are interested in learning more about employee experiences within their own context.
